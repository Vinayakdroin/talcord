Our approach

Two halves of one cord.

Talcord exists at the midpoint of the name itself — talent on one end, the organization on the other, and a deliberate process holding the two together. Every placement starts with connecting; every hire is sustained by harmonizing.

Half one

Connecting talent

We go wide before we go deep — building a talent pool from communities and campuses many recruiters never reach, then reading every job description closely enough to know exactly who in that pool belongs there.

  • Wide, CSR-rooted sourcing network
  • Job descriptions studied, not skimmed
  • Shortlists built on fit, not just résumés
Half two

Harmonizing growth

A match on paper isn't a match on day one. We train each candidate against the real shape of the role, then stay close after placement so the fit holds — and keeps improving for both sides.

  • Role-specific training, not generic onboarding
  • Continuous coaching toward "their better version"
  • Support that continues past the offer letter
How a placement happens

One cord, four points of contact.

The same line runs through every placement we make — it just touches down at different points along the way.

01 / source

Map the role

We sit with your job description until we understand the job, not just the title — the team, the pace, the gap it's meant to close.

02 / train

Shape the talent

Candidates from our pool are trained specifically against that role — skills, expectations, and the soft edges that resumes never show.

03 / match

Send the fit

You meet a shortlist that's already been measured against your role, not a stack of CVs you have to filter yourself.

04 / sustain

Stay in the loop

We check in after placement, on both sides, so the fit keeps holding — and the talent keeps becoming a better version of themselves.
